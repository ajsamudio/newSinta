Sinta by Marie's Story

Once upon a time, in the quiet corners of a sun-drenched studio, Sinta was born from a desire to capture the ephemeral beauty of the world.

Every piece tells a story of a moment noticed, a memory cherished, or a dream whispered. Our process is slow, intentional, and deeply personal.

We believe that jewelry should be more than an accessory; it should be a companion on your journey, a reminder of your own unique story and the magic that exists in the everyday.

Thank you for being part of our story.

Art That Gives Back

Sinta by Marie proudly supports "Marie's Little Library," a global initiative that brings books and art supplies to children around the world.

Because stories, like jewelry, are meant to be shared.

Marie's Little Library

Our mission is to foster creativity and a love for reading in underserved communities. A portion of every Sinta purchase goes directly towards building small libraries and providing art kits to children.

We believe that every child deserves the tools to tell their own story.

Thank you for helping us make a difference.
